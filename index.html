NanoPrint

Privacy Policy  ·  Flying Penguin  ·  Effective date: May 13, 2026

NanoPrint does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server or third party. All data entered in the app stays entirely on your device. Payment processing for optional in-app purchases is handled entirely by Google Play.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how NanoPrint ("the app"), developed by Flying Penguin ("we", "us"), handles information when you use the app on your Android device. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users of NanoPrint worldwide.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, and no data is sent to any external server controlled by us.

The following information is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences system:

  • Text entered for print labels and number cards
  • Images selected for printing
  • Bluetooth printer MAC address
  • App settings and preferences
  • Queued QR codes
  • Pro unlock purchase status (verified via Google Play, stored on-device)

This data is used solely to provide the app's functionality and persists only so your settings are remembered between sessions. It is never transmitted outside your device by us.

3. Bluetooth Usage

NanoPrint uses your device's Bluetooth capability solely to communicate with a compatible thermal printer that you own and operate. The app requests the following Bluetooth-related permissions:

  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT – to connect to and communicate with your printer
  • BLUETOOTH_SCAN – to discover nearby Bluetooth devices (Android 12 and above)
  • BLUETOOTH / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION – for Bluetooth discovery on older Android versions (Android 11 and below)

These permissions are used exclusively to enable printing. No Bluetooth scan results, device names, or MAC addresses are transmitted outside your device.

4. Camera and Storage

NanoPrint may request access to your device's photo library to allow you to select images for printing. No images are copied, uploaded, or shared — they are read locally for the sole purpose of rendering a print preview and sending print data to your Bluetooth printer.

5. Text-to-Speech

NanoPrint uses Android's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to read drawn numbers aloud. This feature operates entirely on-device and does not transmit any data externally.

6. In-App Purchases

NanoPrint offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock Pro features. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information, credit card details, or any financial data.

When you make a purchase, Google Play confirms the transaction to the app so it can enable the unlocked features. This confirmation is stored locally on your device. Google's handling of payment data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

7. Third-Party Services

NanoPrint does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The only external service the app interacts with is Google Play Billing, solely for the purpose of processing and verifying in-app purchases as described in Section 6. No usage data or personal information is shared with any third party by us.

8. Children's Privacy

NanoPrint does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect personal data from any user, regardless of age.

9. Data Security

Since NanoPrint stores all data locally on your device and transmits nothing externally, the security of your data is protected by your device's own security measures, including screen lock and device encryption.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or hold any of your personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All locally stored app data can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app. For data held by Google in relation to purchases, please refer to Google's privacy controls.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
